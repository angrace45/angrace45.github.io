Corrie Ten Boom

Corrie ten Boom is someone I look up to. She is a great example of sacrificial love and forgiveness.

Corrie ten Boom (1892-1983) helped save Jews during the Holocaust sacrificing her safety for others. In 1944, Corrie was arrested for hiding Jews in her home and was sent to a concentration camp. After being released from the concentration camps, she forgave the people who had caused her so much pain.
